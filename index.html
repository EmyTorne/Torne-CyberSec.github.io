CYBER SECURITY

It is the protection of Computer System or Network from information disclosure, theft of, or damage to their hardware, software, or electronic data.

The Function of of Cybersecurity is to identify, protect, detect, response and recover aid organizations in their effort to spot, manage and counter the cyber security threats.

THREATS IN CYBER SECURITY

Any potential harmful assault that aims to get data illegall, interferee online transactions, or contaminate information is referred to as a cyber security threats.

CYBER SECURITY MANAGEMENT STRATEGIES

identifying risk evaluating the organizations environment to identify current or potential risks that could affect.

Assess risk analyzing identified risks to see how likely they are to impact the organization, and what the impact.

Control risk define methods, procedures, technologies, or other measures that can help the organization mitigate.

Review controls evaluating, on an ongoing basis, how effective controls are.

MALWARE

Malware is any software intentionally designed to cause disruption to a computer,server,or computer network,leak private information,gain unauthorized access to information or system.

PHISHING

Phishing is a type of social engineering where attackers sends a fraudulent message designed to trick a person into revealing sensitive information to attacker or deply malicious software on victim infrasture like ransomware.

DENIAL OF SERVICE

Denial of service of service is a type of cyber attack desgned to disable, shutdown or disrupt a network, website or services.

SQL INJECTIONS

SQL injection is a type of code used to attack data-driven application, which malicious SQL statements are inserted into an entry field for execution.

MAN IN THE MIDDLE

Man in the Middle is a type of cyber attack which the attacker secretly intercept between with the two parties or person who believe they are communicating directly with each other.

ZERO DAY EXPLOIT

A Zer Day is a computer-software vulnerability previosly unknown to those who should be interested in its mitigation, like vendor of the target software. Until the vulnerabilty mitigated hackers can exploit it to adversely affect programs, data, additional computer network.

PASSWORD ATTACK

In cryptanalysis and computer security. password cracking is the process of recovering passwords fron data that has been stored in or transmitted by a computer system in scrambled form

CROSS-SITE SCRIPTING

Cross-site Scripting is a type of security vulnerability that can be found in some web application. which an attacker injects malicious executable script into the code of trusted application or website

ROOTKITS

Rootkit is a collection computer software, typically malicious, designed to enable access to a computer or an area of its software that is not ohterwide allowed and often mask its existense or existense of other software

INTERNET OF THINGS ATTACK

The internet of things describe physical objects with sensors, processing ability. software and other technologies that connect and exchange data with other devices and systems over the internet or other communication networks.
